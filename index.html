Biography

I am currently a Research Fellow at the MMLab@NTU, S-Lab, and working with Prof. Chen Change Loy, with the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore.

Before joining NTU, I obtained the PhD degree in 2022 from China and was supervised by Prof. Jun Wang. Meanwhile, I worked closely with Prof. Mingqiang Wei. From 2020 to 2022, I was a Research Assistant at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) and Hong Kong Center for Logistics Robotics.

My research interests include 3D vision, 3D measurement and learning-based point cloud processing.
